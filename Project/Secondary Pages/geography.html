Welcome to your guide to Spain's Geography


Spain is a country with a diversity of geographical monuments. From its castles, its snow-covered mountains and to its lively cities. Spain provides a diversity of different things to do that will satisfy even the pickiest of tourists. Spain is in the extreme southwestern tip of Europe. Just at the border of Africa. Spain occupies about 85% of the Iberian Peninsula with its smaller neighbor Portugal.
